Kho-Kho...

Kho-Kho is a traditional Indian tag game played between two teams of twelve players, with nine active players on the field. It requires speed, agility, and teamwork, as players chase and tag opponents while following specific running and tagging rules. The game is popular in schools and national-level competitions, promoting fitness and strategy.


Cricket

Cricket is a popular bat-and-ball sport played between two teams of eleven players each. The game is known for its different formats, including Test matches, One Day Internationals (ODIs), and Twenty20 (T20). It is widely followed, especially in countries like India, Australia, and England, with major tournaments like the ICC Cricket World Cup.


Football

Football, also known as soccer, is a globally popular sport played between two teams of eleven players each. The game is played on a rectangular field, where players aim to score goals by kicking the ball into the opponent's net. It is known for its fast pace, teamwork, and major tournaments like the FIFA World Cup and UEFA Champions League.
